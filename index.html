Products & Services

Customers & Market

Business Model

B2B Wholesale Only - Louvolite is a wholesaler and sells exclusively to the trade. They do not sell directly to consumers.

Customer Types

  • Blind Retailers & Fabricators - Accredited stockists and dealers who sell to end consumers
  • Commercial Contractors - For office buildings, schools, healthcare facilities
  • Hospitality Sector - Hotels, restaurants, and entertainment venues
  • Residential Sector - Through retail partners for home installations
  • Architects & Specifiers - For commercial and large-scale projects

Market Reach

Global Distribution - Louvolite supplies over 100 countries worldwide through their international network.

Export History

Louvolite began exploring export markets in 1973, initially supplying customers in Australia, New Zealand, South Africa, and the Middle East. Since 1975, the company has regularly exhibited at international trade fairs and leading exhibitions, creating inroads to markets across Europe and Asia.
